Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

You can print using the HZ printers by connecting through the university's print service. Make sure your HZ student card is activated.

2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Use the HZ multifunction printers. Choose "Scan to Email", log in with your HZ account, and the scanned file will be sent directly to your inbox.

3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

Go to the HZ webshop, log in with your student account, select the event or product, and pay online using iDeal or another available method.

4. How can you book a project space?

Booking a project space is done via the HZ Portal or MyHZ. Select the desired time slot and location, then confirm with your HZ account.

5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Register your license plate in the HZ system beforehand. Then you can enter the parking lot using the automated gate linked to your student account.
